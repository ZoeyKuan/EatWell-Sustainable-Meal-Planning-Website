FAQ

Question Answer:
What is EatWell? EatWell is a meal planning platform that helps you discover recipes, create customized meal plans, and shop for ingredients. We prioritize both convenience and sustainability, offering a variety of meal options, including environmentally-friendly choices that align with your dietary preferences.
How does the meal planning feature work? Our meal planning feature lets you plan meals for an entire week, select recipes based on your preferences, and track any dietary restrictions or allergies. You can personalize your plan by choosing meals that cater to your specific needs—whether it's vegetarian, gluten-free, or tailored for your specific health goals.
Can I select meals based on my allergies? Yes! When setting up your account, you can specify any allergies you have. Our system will filter out recipes with ingredients that could trigger your allergies, ensuring that you only see safe meal options. You can also modify your preferences anytime in your account settings.
Are there environmentally-friendly meal options? Absolutely! We believe in offering sustainable choices for our customers. In addition to our regular meal options, we feature recipes that use organic, locally-sourced ingredients or focus on plant-based, lower-carbon footprint meals. These options are perfect for anyone looking to make more environmentally-conscious food choices.
