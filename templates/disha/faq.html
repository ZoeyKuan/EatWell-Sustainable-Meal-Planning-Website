Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is the Meal Planning App?

Our app helps users plan meals, track ingredients, and create shopping lists efficiently.

2. How do I create a meal plan?

You can create a meal plan by selecting recipes from our database and adding them to your weekly schedule.

3. Can I customize my meal plans?

Yes! You can adjust portion sizes, swap ingredients, and personalize meals based on dietary preferences.

4. Does the app support dietary restrictions?

Absolutely! You can filter meals based on dietary preferences such as vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and more.

5. Is the app free to use?

We offer a free version with essential features and a premium plan for advanced meal planning tools.
